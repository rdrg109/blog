1 Introduction

This blog post presents two Youtube playlists which I find valuable for someone learning Computer Science.

I've mainly created this blog post to share this Youtube playlist with my colleagues (undergraduate computer science students).

2 Youtube playlists

The Youtube playlists, which I'd like to share, are published by the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM)

  • ACM A.M. Turing Award Laureate Interviews
  • ACM A.M. Turing Award Lectures

I've noticed that there are some videos which contain very few visits. The Data mining section presents the number of views for each video in those two playlists. The Usefulness of the videos section under the Personal thoughts section presents my thoughts on why I think these playlists are valuable.
